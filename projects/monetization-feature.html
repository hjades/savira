Innovative Monetization Feature

Creating Interactive Charging Activities to Boost Revenue

Strategic Opportunity

Our revenue data showed that while our game had healthy conversion rates (free-to-paying users), the average transaction value was below industry benchmarks. We identified a 20% revenue growth opportunity by increasing transaction frequency and average value without affecting our conversion funnel.

This project directly supported our annual objective to increase ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) by 15% while maintaining or improving retention metrics.

Market Research

I conducted a competitive analysis of top-grossing games in our category, examining:

  • Transaction flow and friction points
  • Psychological hooks and reward mechanisms
  • Cultural preferences in Indonesian market
  • Gamification elements in payment processes

Using SurveyMonkey and in-game polls, we gathered insights from 500+ players about their spending preferences and motivations.

A/B Testing Strategy

We implemented a structured A/B testing approach to validate our hypotheses:

Control (A)

Standard purchase flow with no gamification elements

Conversion Rate: 4.2%
Avg. Transaction: $8.50
User Satisfaction: 72%

Bonus Wheel (B)

Standard wheel-of-fortune style bonus after purchase

Conversion Rate: 4.1%
Avg. Transaction: $9.20
User Satisfaction: 76%

Pachinko Board (C) WINNER

Interactive pachinko-style bonus game after purchase

Conversion Rate: 4.3%
Avg. Transaction: $10.80
User Satisfaction: 88%

Based on these results, we proceeded with the Pachinko Board variant, which not only increased average transaction value but also slightly improved conversion rates and significantly boosted user satisfaction.

Technical Implementation

Implementing the pachinko feature presented several technical challenges:

  • Physics Engine Integration: We needed a lightweight physics simulation for the pachinko balls that wouldn't impact app performance. I worked with engineering to find the right balance between visual fidelity and performance.
  • Cross-Platform Consistency: Ensuring the feature worked identically across Android, iOS, and web required careful planning. We implemented progressive enhancement strategies to optimize for each platform's capabilities.
  • Backend Integration: The bonus currency system needed secure server-side validation to prevent exploitation. I collaborated with our security team to establish a robust verification system.
  • Asset Optimization: The feature required new visual assets, which we optimized to minimize additional app size and loading times.

We addressed these challenges by creating a dedicated cross-functional sprint team with representatives from frontend, backend, QA, and design to ensure cohesive implementation.

Stakeholder Management

This project required alignment across multiple business functions:

  • Finance Team Concerns: Worried about giving away too much bonus currency and impacting revenue. I presented detailed financial models showing the projected net positive impact on revenue despite the bonus giveaways.
  • Marketing Alignment: Needed to coordinate the feature launch with ongoing marketing campaigns. We collaborated on a joint launch strategy that integrated the feature into our seasonal marketing calendar.
  • Executive Buy-In: Some executives questioned the investment in a "game within a game." I presented competitive analysis and market research that demonstrated the proven success of similar mechanisms in top-grossing games.
  • Legal Compliance: Worked with legal to ensure the feature complied with gambling regulations in all our markets. We implemented region-specific adjustments to meet varied legal requirements.

I created a detailed communication plan with regular updates to all stakeholders throughout the development process, which helped maintain alignment and address concerns proactively.
